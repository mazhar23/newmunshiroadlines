New Munshi Road Lines is a premium pan-India road logistics company founded and run by Tanvir Khatri from Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Born out of a vision to make freight movement reliable, transparent, and on-time, we have grown from a regional carrier into a full-scale national logistics force.

Operating from our Ahmedabad headquarters with a key Rajasthan branch, we manage 25+ owned trucks daily while simultaneously running a vetted commission-truck network — giving us the scale to say yes when others say no.

"We don't just move cargo — we move India's economy, one delivery at a time."

From heavy industrial machinery in Gujarat to textiles in Rajasthan, FMCG in Maharashtra to auto parts in Pune — our network knows every highway, every state border, every shortcut that matters.

Ask on WhatsApp
What is the minimum load for LTL booking? +
We accept LTL loads starting from as little as 100 kg. Whether you have a few boxes or a few tonnes, we can consolidate your freight with other loads on the same route — keeping costs very competitive for you.
Do you provide GPS tracking for shipments? +
Yes — all our owned trucks are GPS-equipped. For commission trucks we coordinate with partners who also use tracking. You can call or WhatsApp Tanvir Khatri at any time for a live location update on your cargo.
Which states do you cover for delivery? +
We cover all 28 states and 8 union territories of India through our owned fleet and commission network. No destination is out of reach — from Kashmir to Kanyakumari, Dwarka to Arunachal Pradesh.
How do I get a freight quote? +
You can get a quote in minutes! Call or WhatsApp Tanvir Khatri on +91 78786 97737 with your pickup city, delivery city, cargo type, and approximate weight. You'll have a price within minutes — no lengthy forms needed.
Do you handle transit insurance? +
Yes, we can arrange transit insurance coverage for your shipments. We strongly recommend insuring high-value goods. Our team will guide you through the process at the time of booking.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfers (NEFT/RTGS), UPI payments (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and cash. For regular corporate clients, credit-based billing arrangements can be discussed with Tanvir Khatri directly.
